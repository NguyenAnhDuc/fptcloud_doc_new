# Enable/disable VPN connection

## Disable a VPN connection

1. Select **Disable VPN connection** from the Action menu.

[![Action menu](/img/migrated/image-1744269955705-6bd15bad.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744269955705-6bd15bad.png)

2. In the dialog box, click **Disable VPN connection** to confirm, or **Cancel** to abort.

[![Confirm disable](/img/migrated/image-1744185325093-84bb6317.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744185325093-84bb6317.png)

:::warning
Disabling a VPN connection may take some time. Wait until the process completes before performing further actions.
:::

3. Verify the VPN connection status after disabling.

[![Disabled status](/img/migrated/image-1744269968890-4a79ced3.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744269968890-4a79ced3.png)

## Enable a VPN connection

1. Select **Enable VPN connection** from the Action menu.

[![Action menu](/img/migrated/image-1744269980695-4e2933aa.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744269980695-4e2933aa.png)

2. In the dialog box, click **Enable VPN connection** to confirm, or **Cancel** to abort.

[![Confirm enable](/img/migrated/image-1744185386777-28068112.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744185386777-28068112.png)

:::warning
Enabling a VPN connection may take some time. Wait until the process completes before performing further actions.
:::

3. Verify the VPN connection status after enabling.

[![Enabled status](/img/migrated/image-1744269992510-e4a6ef08.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744269992510-e4a6ef08.png)
