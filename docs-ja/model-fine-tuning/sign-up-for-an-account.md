# Sign Up For An Account

### 1. For the PAYG user
Create an FPT Cloud account at <https://ai.fptcloud.com/> or <https://ai.fptcloud.jp/>, click **Sign up** and fill in the required information to register an account. Then, check your email for instructions from FPT Smart Cloud (noreply@fptcloud.com) to complete the registration and verify your account.
[![file](/img/migrated/Sign-up-debdda1c.png)](/img/migrated/Sign-up-debdda1c.png)
In case you have an account, you sign in to the **FPT AI Factory Portal** at <https://ai.fptcloud.com/> or <https://ai.fptcloud.jp/>, with your FPT Cloud account and password. Then, you need to verify from your device.
[![file](/img/migrated/Sign-in-c414053f.png)](/img/migrated/Sign-in-c414053f.png)
After signing in successfully, you will be directed to the **AI Factory Portal**.
[![file](/img/migrated/AI-Factory-Portal-e041e9d7.png)](/img/migrated/AI-Factory-Portal-e041e9d7.png)

### 2. For the user to sign a contract
Create an FPT Cloud account at <https://ai.fptcloud.com/> or <https://ai.fptcloud.jp/>, click **Sign up** and fill in the required information to register an account. Then, check your email for instructions from FPT Smart Cloud (noreply@fptcloud.com) to complete the registration and verify your account.
[![file](/img/migrated/Sign-up-debdda1c.png)](/img/migrated/Sign-up-debdda1c.png)
Wait for Tenant Provisioning
  * FPT will provide a tenant for your account.
  * **Notice:** Ensure the user is added to the tenant with an AI Studio quota, in the Hanoi2 region, and has access to the Model Hub. In case user needs to use a new Tenant exclusively, just contact the Support team (hotline: 1900 638 399 or email: support@fptcloud.com) to be granted a Tenant quota.
  * You will receive a notification via emails once the tenant is ready.

Once you receive the notification, go to the sign in page <https://ai.fptcloud.com/> or <https://ai.fptcloud.jp/.Then>, you need to verify from your device.
[![Alt text](/img/migrated/Sign-in-c414053f.png)](/img/migrated/Sign-in-c414053f.png)
After signing in successfully, **switch** to your provisioned tenant, which has been sent via email by FPT, to use Model Fine-tuning.
[![Alt text](/img/migrated/Switch-tenant-ef56c0e2.png)](/img/migrated/Switch-tenant-ef56c0e2.png)
