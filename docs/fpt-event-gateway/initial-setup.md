# Initial Setup

To start using the **FPT Event Gateway** service, you need to log in to the FPT Portal.

Visit <https://console.fptcloud.com> and log in with your FPT Cloud account credentials. Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC for the service you want to use.

If you do not yet have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at <https://fptcloud.com/>.

Then select the **Sign Up** function and enter your information as guided by the system. Our support team will contact you shortly to confirm your details and create your account.

If you encounter any errors during the process, please contact our Support team immediately for assistance.
