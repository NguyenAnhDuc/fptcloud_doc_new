# Download detailed reconciliation file

To download the detailed reconciliation data as an Excel file:

1. After selecting the billing period, contract, and reconciliation entry, click the **Download** button.

   [![Download button](/img/migrated/image-1744088593606-63e60ab6.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744088593606-63e60ab6.png)

2. The reconciliation file will be sent to your email address (the email associated with the logged-in account) within 15–20 minutes.

   [![Email confirmation for download](/img/migrated/image-1744088600025-5d3582dc.png)](/img/migrated/image-1744088600025-5d3582dc.png)

:::warning
The system accepts Download requests at a rate of once every 15 minutes. Please do not click Download multiple times within a 15-minute window.
:::
