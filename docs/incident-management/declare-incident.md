# Declare an incident directly

1. From the left menu, click **Apps** > **Incident**.
2. Click **Declare Incident**.
3. Enter the incident information:
   - **What's going on?**: describe or name the ongoing incident
   - **Severity**: severity level of the incident
   - **Status**: current status of the incident
   - **Labels**: list of labels assigned to the incident
4. Click **Declare incident** to complete.
