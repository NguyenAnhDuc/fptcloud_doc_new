# Initial setup

If this is your first time using FPT ASPM v1.0, please check and complete the following steps first.

## Create an FPT Cloud account and log in to the FPT Portal

To start using the FSP DevSec Service, you need to log in to the FPT Portal. Go to [https://console.fptcloud.com](https://console.fptcloud.com) and sign in with your FPT Cloud credentials. Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC for the service you want to use.

If you do not yet have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at [https://fptcloud.com/](https://fptcloud.com/). Select **Sign Up** and enter the required information as guided by the system. Our support team will contact you shortly to verify your information and create your account.

If you encounter any errors during setup, contact our Support team immediately for assistance.
