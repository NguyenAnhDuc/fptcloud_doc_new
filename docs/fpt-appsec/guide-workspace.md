# Workspace management

**Step 1:** Click **Workspace** in the menu.

**Step 2:** Select the team you want to work with.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/1-52d0b798.png)](/img/migrated/1-52d0b798.png)

The screen displays a list of the team's assets with information such as **Status**, **Last request time**, and **Issues Severity**. You can perform actions such as Scan/Rescan, Disable/Enable, and Delete. You can also filter assets by **Name**, **Severity**, and **Recently Imported**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/w2-bde88680.png)](/img/migrated/w2-bde88680.png)
