# Edit organization information

Only **Org Admin** has permission to edit.

**Step 1:** Click the sub-tab, then select **Organization**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od1-c5e2421f.png)](/img/migrated/od1-c5e2421f.png)

**Step 2:** Click **Settings** in the **Organization** section. You can then edit the organization's information.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od2-0ef111f4.png)](/img/migrated/od2-0ef111f4.png)

Here you can view fields such as **Code**, **Org Name**, and **Description**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od3-new-5f772255.png)](/img/migrated/od3-new-5f772255.png)

**Step 3:** Click **Edit** in the **Information** tab.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od4-69f817e8.png)](/img/migrated/od4-69f817e8.png)

**Step 4:** Fill in the **Org Name** and **Org Description** fields.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od5-0623354b.png)](/img/migrated/od5-0623354b.png)

**Step 5:** Click **Save**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/od6-0ead19d4.png)](/img/migrated/od6-0ead19d4.png)
