# Manage team members

In the **Organization** section, click **Member** to view the member list and available actions.

## Add a member

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Add Member**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/am1-eb13b294.png)](/img/migrated/am1-eb13b294.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in **Org Unit**, **Member**, and **Role**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/am2-f65d7367.png)](/img/migrated/am2-f65d7367.png)

**Step 3:** Click **Save**.

## Change a member's role

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Change Role** in the Action column for a member.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/Cm1-c672b86e.png)](/img/migrated/Cm1-c672b86e.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in **Org Unit**, **Member**, and **Role**.

**Step 3:** Click **Save**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/cm2-75104754.png)](/img/migrated/cm2-75104754.png)

## Remove a member

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Remove** in the Action column for a member.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/R1-1-c27aea16.png)](/img/migrated/R1-1-c27aea16.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in the required information, then click **Confirm**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/SaveRM-1986c195.png)](/img/migrated/SaveRM-1986c195.png)

## Disable a member

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Disable** in the Action column for a member.

## Enable a member

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Enable** in the Action column for a member.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/Enm1-be16f666.png)](/img/migrated/Enm1-be16f666.png)

**Step 2:** Enter the member's **email**, then click **Confirm**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/ENB2-0753575e.png)](/img/migrated/ENB2-0753575e.png)
