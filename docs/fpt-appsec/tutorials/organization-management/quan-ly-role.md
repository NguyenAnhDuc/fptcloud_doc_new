# Manage roles

Only **Org Admin** has permission to edit.

**Step 1:** Click the **Roles** tab in **Organization** to view the list of member roles.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/or1-56b5b42e.png)](/img/migrated/or1-56b5b42e.png)

You can view each role's **Name**, **Type**, **Number of Members**, **Description**, **Status**, and **Created at**, and search by any of these fields.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/R1-0cd5642a.png)](/img/migrated/R1-0cd5642a.png)

**Step 2:** Click the **Role name** hyperlink to view the role's permissions and associated members.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/R2-821fa827.png)](/img/migrated/R2-821fa827.png)

The detail view shows the role's **Name**, **Description**, **Created at**, **Status**, and **Permissions**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/R3-318bc0f9.png)](/img/migrated/R3-318bc0f9.png)
