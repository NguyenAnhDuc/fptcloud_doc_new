# Manage teams

Click **Team** in the **Organization** section.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/ot1-4890d1ae.png)](/img/migrated/ot1-4890d1ae.png)

Here you can view each team's **Name**, **Description**, **Number of Members**, **Status**, and **Created at**, and perform actions such as **Create Team**, **Create Sub-team**, **Edit**, **Disable/Enable**, and **Delete**.

## Create a team

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Create team** in the **Teams** section.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/CT1-9725d6d4.png)](/img/migrated/CT1-9725d6d4.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in **Team Name**, **Description**, and **Parent Org Unit**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/CT2-8455b3c6.png)](/img/migrated/CT2-8455b3c6.png)

**Step 3:** Click **Create**.

## Create a sub-team

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Create Sub-team** in the Action column of a team.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/CST1-411e33b4.png)](/img/migrated/CST1-411e33b4.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in **Org Name**, **Org Description**, and **Parent Org Unit**, then click **Create**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/CST2-6ab10ae3.png)](/img/migrated/CST2-6ab10ae3.png)

## Edit a team

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Edit** in the Action column of a team.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/EDO-d1cc8e5e.png)](/img/migrated/EDO-d1cc8e5e.png)

**Step 2:** Fill in **Org Name**, **Org Description**, and **Parent Org Unit**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/EDO2-285e9fb4.png)](/img/migrated/EDO2-285e9fb4.png)

**Step 3:** Click **Save**.

## Delete a team

Only **Org Admin** and **Team Manager** have permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Delete** in the Action column of a team.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/DETO-9a2f3ce9.png)](/img/migrated/DETO-9a2f3ce9.png)

**Step 2:** Type **delete**, then click **Confirm**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/DETO2-031a46c8.png)](/img/migrated/DETO2-031a46c8.png)

## Disable a team

Only **Org Admin** has permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Disable** in the Action column of a team.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/DTO-7f086bfe.png)](/img/migrated/DTO-7f086bfe.png)

**Step 2:** Type **disable**, then click **Confirm**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/DTO2-fe8efa07.png)](/img/migrated/DTO2-fe8efa07.png)

## Enable a team

Only **Org Admin** has permission.

**Step 1:** Click **Enable** in the Action column of a team.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/En1-1-7958e94c.png)](/img/migrated/En1-1-7958e94c.png)

**Step 2:** Click **Confirm**.

[![anhfsec](/img/migrated/En2-1-570ad3a9.png)](/img/migrated/En2-1-570ad3a9.png)
