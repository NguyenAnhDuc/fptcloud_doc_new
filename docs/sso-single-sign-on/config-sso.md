# Configure SSO

SSO cannot be self-configured on the FPT Cloud system at this time. To use the SSO service, submit a ticket or contact the support team and provide the following information:

- Sub-domain for quick system access. After SSO is configured, you will access FPT Cloud at: `http://sub-domain.fptcloud.com`
- The email address of the account that will be the Tenant owner
- Your Identity Provider for SSO

The technical support team will contact you to request additional internal configuration details.
