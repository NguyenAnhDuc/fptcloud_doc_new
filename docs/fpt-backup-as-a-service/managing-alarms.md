# Managing Alarms

**Step 1:** Login ando trang portal quản trị of service Backup As a Service tại: <https://baas.fptcloud.com> [![file](/img/migrated/image-1695292948402-3616b178.png)](/img/migrated/image-1695292948402-3616b178.png)
**Step 2:** Cửa sổ hiện ra chọn **Configuration**. [![file](/img/migrated/image-1695292954154-840bf510.png)](/img/migrated/image-1695292954154-840bf510.png)
**Step 3:** Select **A**** larms Management**. Sau that chọn Alarm muốn sử dụng and chọn Enable. [![file](/img/migrated/image-1695292963049-43668c28.png)](/img/migrated/image-1695292963049-43668c28.png)
**Step 4:** Bạn chọn **Edit** to can tùy chỉnh lại thông số for phù hợp with nhu cầu of you. [![file](/img/migrated/image-1695292969858-8e06f50e.png)](/img/migrated/image-1695292969858-8e06f50e.png)
