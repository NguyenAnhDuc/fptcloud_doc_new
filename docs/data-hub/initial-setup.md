# Initial Setup

* Create an FPT Cloud account: Visit <https://ai.fptcloud.com/>, click **Sign Up** and fill in the required information to register an account. Then, check your email for instructions from **FPT Smart Cloud** (noreply@fptcloud.com) to complete the registration and verify your account.

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1742891973865-70c602e1.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742891973865-70c602e1.png)
  * Log in to the FPT AI Factory Portal: Go to <https://ai.fptcloud.com/> and sign in with your **FPT Cloud account and password**

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1742891982448-756e7f02.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742891982448-756e7f02.png)
  * After logging in successfully, you will be directed to the AI Factory Portal

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1740036087795-6bc5c01a.png)](/img/migrated/image-1740036087795-6bc5c01a.png)
  * Note: 
    * For the user to sign a contract, FPT will first provide a tenant and invite you to access the FPT AI Factory Portal via emails. In case user need to use a new Tenant exclusively, just contact Support team (Hotline: 1900 638 399 or email: support@fptcloud.com) to be granted a Tenant quota.
    * Ensure the user is added to the tenant with an AI Studio quota, in the SG region, and has access to the model hub.
