# Using SSO

After SSO is configured, you can log in to the system in two ways:

## Method 1: Direct URL

1. Go to: `http://sub-domain.fptcloud.com`
2. Authenticate your login credentials

   [![SSO authentication screen](/img/migrated/image-1734056582232-ddd3fa5b.png)](/img/migrated/image-1734056582232-ddd3fa5b.png)

3. The system authenticates your login and redirects you to the instance management page

## Method 2: Select-method page

1. Go to: `https://console.fptcloud.com/login/select-method`
2. Select **Login with your organization**

   [![Select login method](/img/migrated/image-1734056612511-805a1ea0.png)](/img/migrated/image-1734056612511-805a1ea0.png)

3. Enter your registered sub-domain

   [![Enter sub-domain](/img/migrated/image-1734056636838-3134f1b3.png)](/img/migrated/image-1734056636838-3134f1b3.png)

4. Authenticate your login credentials

   [![Authenticate login](/img/migrated/image-1734056657577-d806a4b5.png)](/img/migrated/image-1734056657577-d806a4b5.png)

5. The system authenticates your login and redirects you to the instance management page
