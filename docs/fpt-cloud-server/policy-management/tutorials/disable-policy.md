# Disable a policy

To use the Disable Policy feature, you must log in to the system with Tenant owner permissions.

**Step 1:** In the menu, select **Policy Management**. Select a policy with **Active** status, then select **Actions** > **Disable policy**.

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1719821801276-005669dd.png)](/img/migrated/image-1719821801276-005669dd.png)

**Step 2:** A dialog box appears. Click **Disable policy** to confirm the action. The system will disable the policy and update its status on the **Policy Management** screen.

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1719821812687-d3ebd4bf.png)](/img/migrated/image-1719821812687-d3ebd4bf.png)

**Step 3:** The system disables the policy and updates its status from **Active** to **Inactive** on the **Policy Management** screen.

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1719821825580-3247e00e.png)](/img/migrated/image-1719821825580-3247e00e.png)
