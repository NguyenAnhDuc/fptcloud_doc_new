Detach a policy from a group

## **Step 1:** Go to **Autoscaling > Autoscale Group**. Click the name of the group you want to detach a policy from.

![](images/detach-policy-khoi-group/img-001.png)

## **Step 2:** Switch to the **Policy** tab. On the row containing the policy you want to detach, click the **Action menu** icon.

![](images/detach-policy-khoi-group/img-002.png)

## **Step 3:** In the menu, select **Detach**.

![](images/detach-policy-khoi-group/img-003.png)

## **Step 4:** A confirmation dialog will appear. Click **Detach** to confirm.
