# General Intro Model Serving

**Purpose & Scope of the Document: **
  * This document provides a guide for customers on using the **FPT Smart Cloud Model Serving** service, including login procedures, usage, and management via the **FPT Portal** interface.

**Overview of the Product:** FPT Smart Cloud officially launches the FPT Model Serving service. With this service, customers can easily deploy AI models onto the FPT Cloud infrastructure. Model Serving offers high availability and low latency for deploying artificial intelligence models. The service automatically scales up or down to meet changing demands, saving infrastructure costs while optimizing latency performance. Model Serving supports a wide range of AI models from various sources, including Private Models, FPT AI Models, NVIDIA NGC, and Custom Models from public hubs such as Hugging Face. Model Serving offers two service models: Deployment and Endpoint.
