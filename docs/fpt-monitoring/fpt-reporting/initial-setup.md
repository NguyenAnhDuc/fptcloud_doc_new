# Initial setup

1. To start using **FPT Cloud Reporting**, log in to the FPT Portal. Go to **https://console.fptcloud.com** and sign in with your FPT Cloud account credentials. Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC for the service.

2. If you do not yet have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at **https://fptcloud.com**, click **Sign Up**, and fill in the required information. The support team will contact you to confirm your details and create your account.

:::tip
If you encounter any errors during setup, contact our Support team immediately for assistance.
:::
