# Test send email

The **Test send email** feature lets you verify report content and confirm that the report is successfully delivered to recipients.

To use this feature:

1. Go to **Home > Apps > Reporting > Create a new report**.
2. Fill in all report details: **Select Dashboard**, **Format Report**, **Schedule**, and **Confirm**.
3. On the **Confirm** screen, click **Send email test**.

   [![Test send email button on the Confirm screen](/img/migrated/Picture10-0a9f7fe5.png)](/img/migrated/Picture10-0a9f7fe5.png)

4. A dialog box appears — enter the test email address, then click **Send**.
5. Check the recipient's inbox to verify the report was received.
