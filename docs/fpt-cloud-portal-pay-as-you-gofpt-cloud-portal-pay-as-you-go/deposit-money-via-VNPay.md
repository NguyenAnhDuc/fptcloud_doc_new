# Top Up via VNPay

You can top up your account balance through the VNPay payment gateway.

1. Go to the **Billing** page and select the **Topup** menu.

[![Billing – Topup page](/img/migrated/image-1694073789071-dcc595d7.png)](/img/migrated/image-1694073789071-dcc595d7.png)

2. Select a preset denomination or enter a custom amount.

[![Select top-up amount](/img/migrated/image-1694073800926-6af15e22.png)](/img/migrated/image-1694073800926-6af15e22.png)

3. Click **Pay via VNPay** and complete the VNPay payment process.

:::note
If the payment is successful, the system will display a confirmation message and credit the amount to your account.
:::

[![Top-up confirmation screen](/img/migrated/image-1694073814699-e6b48ffa.png)](/img/migrated/image-1694073814699-e6b48ffa.png)
