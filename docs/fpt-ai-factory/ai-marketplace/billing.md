# Billing

## How to manage your billing?

1. Navigate to the **My account** dropdown and click **Add to credit balance** or **Buy credit to start using API** to access your billing information and settings.

[![My account dropdown showing Add to credit balance and Buy credit options](/img/migrated/1-1-29c4dda0.png)](/img/migrated/1-1-29c4dda0.png)

2. You can view the pricing for **AI Marketplace — Model as a service**.

[![Pricing page for AI Marketplace Model as a service](/img/migrated/2-b02ccb07.png)](/img/migrated/2-b02ccb07.png)

3. To buy credit, click the **Purchase** button. You can choose to top up with a recommended amount such as **1,500,000 VND** or **2,500,000 VND**, or enter your desired amount. The minimum transaction is **26,000 VND**. Then click **Purchase**.

[![Purchase credit dialog with recommended amounts and custom amount input](/img/migrated/3-973418ce.png)](/img/migrated/3-973418ce.png)

4. You will be redirected to **VNPay** to complete the transaction using your preferred payment method: **VNPayQR** or **international payment cards**.

[![VNPay payment page with QR code and card payment options](/img/migrated/image-1742892528929-dc30d234.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742892528929-dc30d234.png)

5. After a top-up, check **Credit History** to view your top-up transaction history.

[![Credit History page showing top-up transactions with date and amount](/img/migrated/image-1742892550518-3a6ee216.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742892550518-3a6ee216.png)
