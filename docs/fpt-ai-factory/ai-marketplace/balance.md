# Balance

## How to check your account balance?

[![Credit balance displayed at the top of the header navigation](/img/migrated/Picture17-4af9c4e0.png)](/img/migrated/Picture17-4af9c4e0.png)

Your credit balance is shown at the top of the header.

To top up, click **Add credit** and follow the [billing guide](./billing.md).

:::note
This balance is shared across all paid services of AI Factory that you have subscribed to.
:::
