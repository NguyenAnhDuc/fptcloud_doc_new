# Sign In

After creating your account, sign in to the Portal to use the **Model Hub** service.

## Steps

1. Go to <https://console.fptcloud.com>.

2. Sign in with your account credentials.

   > **Note:** Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC.

3. The portal interface appears:
[![](/img/migrated/Picture1-67e96351.png)](/img/migrated/Picture1-67e96351.png)

4. In the menu, select **AI Platform** > **Model Hub**.

The **Model Hub** service screen appears and you can begin using the service.
