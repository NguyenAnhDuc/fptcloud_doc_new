# Completion history

Select a session and open the **Completion History** tab to view all past completions for that session.

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1746697738348.39.04-a9a17fa3.png)](/img/migrated/image-1746697738348.39.04-a9a17fa3.png)

- You can choose your preferred completion from the list or download the full history of text completions.
- Click Continue to test the model using your selected completion.
