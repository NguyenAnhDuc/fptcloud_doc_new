# Add Credit

If your balance is insufficient to create a new GPU container, please top up your account and try again by following these steps:
  1. In the left-side menu of AI Factory Portal, click the **Billing**
  2. You have to **choose a currency** if it is the first time you access Billing. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-39-26-9352e9a8.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-39-26-9352e9a8.png)
  3. Click the **Add credit** button [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-49-23-92aca56f.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-49-23-92aca56f.png)
  4. In the **Add credit pop-up** , you can select a suggested amount or **enter the amount of credits** you want to add to your balance. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-47-16-e08e227b.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-47-16-e08e227b.png)
  5. Then select a **payment method** : 
     * **VNPay** : Only supports the Vietnam region and Vietnamese dongs. You will complete the transaction on the **VNPay** checkout website.
     * **Stripe** : Enter the card details in the pop-up window to proceed with the payment. **All transactions are securely processed by Stripe, and FPT does not store card information.**
[![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-55-30-5be3b48e.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-55-30-5be3b48e.png)
  6. After the payment is completed, a pop-up will appear displaying a success notification, and your balance will be updated accordingly. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/Picture1-9ed4b9be.png)](/img/migrated/Picture1-9ed4b9be.png)
