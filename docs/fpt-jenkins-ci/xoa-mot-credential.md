# Delete a credential

To delete a credential you no longer need:

**Step 1:** In the FPT Portal menu > **Jenkins CI** > **Detail Cluster** > **Credential Managements**.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture62-44b50e96.png)](/img/migrated/Picture62-44b50e96.png)

**Step 2:** On the **Credential Managements** screen, select the credential to delete > **Actions** > **Delete**.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture63-8d44ed2a.png)](/img/migrated/Picture63-8d44ed2a.png)

**Step 3:** Type **delete** and click **Confirm** to confirm the deletion.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture64-3c5f5142.png)](/img/migrated/Picture64-3c5f5142.png)

**Step 4:** Result after deleting the credential on the Portal.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture65-3be2ceb5.png)](/img/migrated/Picture65-3be2ceb5.png)
