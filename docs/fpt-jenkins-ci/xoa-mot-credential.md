# Delete a credential

Để xoá a credential không sử dụng, user thực hiện as follows:
**Step 1.** Tại menu FPT Portal > **Jenkins CI** > Detail Cluster >**Credential Managements:** [![](/img/migrated/Picture62-44b50e96.png)](/img/migrated/Picture62-44b50e96.png) **Step 2.** Tại màn **Credential Managements** > Chọn credential need to xoá > Actions **Delete** : [![](/img/migrated/Picture63-8d44ed2a.png)](/img/migrated/Picture63-8d44ed2a.png) **Step 3.** Enter **delete** > Click **Confirm** to xác nhận hành động xoá: [![](/img/migrated/Picture64-3c5f5142.png)](/img/migrated/Picture64-3c5f5142.png) **Step 4.** Kết quả after xoá credential: [![](/img/migrated/Picture65-3be2ceb5.png)](/img/migrated/Picture65-3be2ceb5.png) Kết quả dưới Jenkins instance: [![](/img/migrated/Picture66-75017d5d.png)](/img/migrated/Picture66-75017d5d.png) [![](/img/migrated/Picture67-3869e873.png)](/img/migrated/Picture67-3869e873.png)
