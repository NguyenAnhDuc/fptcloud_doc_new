# Initial setup

If this is your first time using FPT Jenkins CI, complete the following steps before getting started:

1. Create an FPT Cloud account and log in to the FPT Portal.
2. To use the FPT Jenkins CI service, you need to log in to the FPT Portal.
3. Go to <https://console.fptcloud.com> and log in with your FPT Cloud account credentials. Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC for the service.
4. If you do not have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at <https://fptcloud.com/>.
5. Click **Sign Up** and fill in the information as instructed. Our support team will contact you to confirm account details.

If you encounter any errors during the process, contact our Support team immediately for assistance.
