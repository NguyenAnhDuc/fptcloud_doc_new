# What is Model Testing – Interactive Sessions

**Model Testing – Interactive Sessions** is the critical next step after fine-tuning. It is designed to validate and evaluate how your customized AI model performs in real-world scenarios. Built into FPT AI Factory Portal, it lets you interact directly with your fine-tuned model through a simple and intuitive interface.

[![Alt text](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Playground-Interactive-Session-1.png)](https://fptcloud.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Playground-Interactive-Session-1.png)

Rather than relying solely on static metrics like accuracy or loss, Interactive Sessions provides a dynamic environment where you can:

- **Chat with the model in natural language**.
- **Test domain-specific queries** to evaluate contextual understanding.
- **Assess response quality** based on relevance, tone, and completeness.
- **Iterate quickly** by adjusting prompts and observing model behavior in real time.

This hands-on approach ensures the model delivers meaningful, accurate output suited to your business context — whether in customer service, healthcare, finance, or legal — not just good numbers on paper.

Through seamless integration with Model Fine-tuning, **Model Testing – Interactive Sessions** empowers organizations to deploy powerful, practical AI solutions with confidence. It bridges the gap from training to production, enabling your team to make informed decisions before scaling the model.
