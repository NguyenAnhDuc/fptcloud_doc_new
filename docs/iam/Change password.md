# Change Password

After IAM User successfully registers and logs in to FPT Portal or after a period of use, the User should change the password to ensure the security of the account.
**Step 1** : Click the area where the account name is displayed in the upper right corner of FPT Portal > Select **Change Password**.
[![](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-27-1024x285-88368ee2.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-27-1024x285-88368ee2.png)
**Step 2** : On the interface of the **Change Password** , enter the required information:
  * **Current** : Enter your current password.
  * **New** : Enter your new password.
  * **Confirm** : Confirm that the password is now new.[![](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-28-c40f7554.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-28-c40f7554.png)

**Step 3:** Select**Change Password** , the system will check and process and notify the results.
[![](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-29-7da35c1e.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-FPT-IAM-2022-29-7da35c1e.png)
