# Billing

## How to manage your billing?
  * Navigate to the My account dropdown and click on **Add to credit balance** or **Buy credit to start using API** to access your billing information and settings [![file](/img/migrated/1-1-29c4dda0.png)](/img/migrated/1-1-29c4dda0.png)
  * You can view the pricing for **AI Marketplace - Model as a service** [![file](/img/migrated/2-b02ccb07.png)](/img/migrated/2-b02ccb07.png)
  * To buy credit, click **Purchase** button. You can choose to top up with the recommended amount, such as **1,500,000 VND** or **2,500,000 VND** , or enter your desired amount. The minimum transaction is **26,000 VND,** then click "**Purchase** ". [![file](/img/migrated/3-973418ce.png)](/img/migrated/3-973418ce.png)
  * You will be redirected to the **VNPay** payment to complete the transaction with your selected amount using your preferred payment methods: **VNPayQR** or **International payment cards** ,...

[![file](/img/migrated/image-1742892528929-dc30d234.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742892528929-dc30d234.png)
  * After a top-up, check **Credit History** to view your top-up transaction history. [![file](/img/migrated/image-1742892550518-3a6ee216.png)](/img/migrated/image-1742892550518-3a6ee216.png)
