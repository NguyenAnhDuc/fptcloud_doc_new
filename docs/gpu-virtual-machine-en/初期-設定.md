# Initial Setup

If you are using the service for the first time, complete the following steps before getting started.

  * **Create an FPT Cloud account.** If you do not yet have an account, go to <https://fptcloud.com/>, click **Sign Up**, and follow the system instructions to enter your details. Our support team will contact you shortly to verify your information and activate your account.
  * **Log in to the FPT Portal.** Go to <https://console.fptcloud.com> and sign in with your **FPT Cloud account and password**. Make sure to select the correct **Tenant** and **Region**.
  * **Set up an SSH key:** Navigate to **SSH Management** to generate an SSH key. This key will be used for secure access to your servers.
  * **Help:** If you encounter any issues during the process, contact the **Support Team**.
