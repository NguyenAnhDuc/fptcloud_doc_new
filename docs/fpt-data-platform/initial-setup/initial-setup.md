# Initial Setup

To start using the **FPT Cloud Data Platform** service, you need to log in to the FPT Portal.

Visit <https://console.fptcloud.com> and sign in with your FPT Cloud account credentials. Select the correct Tenant, Region, and VPC for the service you want to use.

If you do not have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at <https://fptcloud.com/>.

Then select the **Sign Up** function and enter the required information as instructed by the system. Our support team will contact you shortly after to verify your information and create your account.

If you encounter any errors during the process, please contact our Support team immediately for assistance.
