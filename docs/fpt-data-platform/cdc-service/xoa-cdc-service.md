# Delete CDC Service

**To delete a CDC Service, follow these steps:**

 * **Step 1:** In the menu bar, select **Data Platform** > **Workspace Management** > **Workspace name**

 * **Step 2:** In the **My services** section, select **CDC service**

You can also navigate directly to the CDC service by:

In the menu bar, select **Data Platform** > **CDC service** > click a **CDC service name**

 * **Step 3:** On the **Detail CDC service** screen > select **CDC service name** > select **Action** > click **Delete** ![image-1](images/xoa-cdc-service/img-001.png)

 * **Step 4:** The **Delete Application** dialog appears

→ Enter **delete** > click **Confirm** to remove the service from the workspace

→ Click **Cancel** to cancel the operation ![image-2](images/xoa-cdc-service/img-002.png)
