# Configure email notifications

FPT Cloud allows you to configure email notifications for your Jenkins instance through the Portal interface.

### Edit email configuration

**Step 1:** In the FPT Portal menu > **Jenkins CI** > **Detail Cluster** > **Configurations** > **Edit** email.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture68-f44a375a.png)](/img/migrated/Picture68-f44a375a.png)

**Step 2:** Enter the updated email configuration details and click **Update**.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture69-333db764.png)](/img/migrated/Picture69-333db764.png)

**Step 3:** Result after updating the email configuration.

[![](/img/migrated/Picture70-a6668456.png)](/img/migrated/Picture70-a6668456.png)
