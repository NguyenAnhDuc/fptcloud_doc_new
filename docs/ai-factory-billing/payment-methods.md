# Payment Methods

FPT AI Factory uses **PCI-certified partners** for all payment processing.  
| **Payment Method**  | **Region**  | **Supported Currency**  |  
| --- | --- | --- |  
| VNPAY  | Vietnam  | VND (Vietnamese dong)  |  
| PayPal  | Vietnam  | USD (U.S. dollars  |  
| Stripe  | Vietnam  | USD (U.S. dollars), VND (Vietnamese dong)  |  
| Stripe  | Japan  | USD (U.S. dollars), JPY (Japanese yen)  |  
> All your payment information is securely stored by **VNPAY** , **PayPal** and **Stripe**. FPT AI Factory **does not store any payment information**.
