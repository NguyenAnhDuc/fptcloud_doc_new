# Add credits

To start using the AI Factory services with the PAYG billing plan, you need to buy credits first.
Follow the instructions below:
  1. In the left-side menu, click **Billing** under the **Account** section
  2. Click the **Add credit** button [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-49-23-c0ffac4e.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-22_11-49-23-c0ffac4e.png)
  3. In the **Add Credit pop-up** , you can select a suggested amount or **enter the amount of credits you** want to add to your balance. *_A minimum amount is required—you can't add less._ [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-7-6_17-32-55-29e3537a.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-7-6_17-32-55-29e3537a.png)
  4. Then select a **payment method** :
     * **VNPay** : Only supports the Vietnam region and Vietnamese dongs. You will complete the transaction on the VNPay checkout website.
     * **PayPal** : Only supports the Vietnam region and US dollar. You will complete the transaction on the PayPal checkout pop-up.
     * **Stripe** : Enter the card details in the pop-up window to proceed with the payment. **All transactions are securely processed by Stripe, and FPT does not store card information.**

An OTP may be required to verify the payment transaction. It depends on your card issuer. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-7-6_17-34-54-77174d1a.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-7-6_17-34-54-77174d1a.png) [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-8-10_11-23-14-86accd6e.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-8-10_11-23-14-86accd6e.png)
  5. After the payment is completed, a pop-up will appear displaying a success notification, and your **Total Balance** and **Top-up Balance** will be updated accordingly. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/Picture1-1-9f3038ad.png)](/img/migrated/Picture1-1-9f3038ad.png)
  6. You can also check out your adding credit transaction in the **Credit history** table. [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-28_12-17-42-eecb0352.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-28_12-17-42-eecb0352.png)
