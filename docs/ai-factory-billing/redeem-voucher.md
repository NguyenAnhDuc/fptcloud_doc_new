# Redeem Voucher

To start using AI Factory services, you may use **Top-up Credits** or **Voucher Credits**.
If you have received a **voucher code** —for example, as part of a promotional offer—you can apply it to redeem credits!
## Notice
  * The voucher must be **redeemed within a validity period**. Expired codes cannot be used.
  * **Voucher credits also expire after** **a validity period** and **cannot be used once expired.**
  * **If you have both top-up and voucher credits,** the system will deduct voucher credits first when using AI Factory services.

## Add a voucher
After you have received a voucher code, follow the instructions below:
  1. In the left-side menu, click **Billing** under the **Account** section
  2. In the **Add Voucher** area, enter your voucher code and click **Apply Code**. (_Note: Voucher codes consist of 10 characters, including uppercase letters and numbers.)_ [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-30_13-37-5-94c9066a.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-30_13-37-5-94c9066a.png)
  3. Once applied successfully, a confirmation message will appear: "**Your voucher has been applied successfully.** " [![Alt text](/img/migrated/image2025-6-30_13-56-37-f36f6894.png)](/img/migrated/image2025-6-30_13-56-37-f36f6894.png)
  4. The applied voucher will appear under the **Voucher List** **tab** for your reference.
