# Overview

## What is AI Factory Billing how does it work?
**AI Factory Billing** allows customers to access our services through a pay-as-you-go model with convenient online payments, offering a fully self-service experience. 
## How does it work?
To get started, you need to purchase credits to top up your balance. Once your balance has sufficient credits, you can immediately begin using AI Factory services.
To estimate the amount of credits you may need, please refer to the pricing pages:
  * Vietnam region – [ai.fptcloud.com/pricing](http://ai.fptcloud.com/pricing)
  * Japan region – [ai.fptcloud.jp/pricing](http://ai.fptcloud.jp/pricing)
