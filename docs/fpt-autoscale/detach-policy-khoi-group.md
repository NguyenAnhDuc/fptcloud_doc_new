# Detach Policy from Group

## Step 1: Go to Autoscaling > Autoscale Group. Click the name of the group you want to detach a policy from.

[![open group details](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)

## Step 2: Switch to the **Policy** tab. On the row containing the policy to detach, click the **Action menu** icon.

[![policy action menu](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)

## Step 3: In the menu, select **Detach**.

[![detach policy menu option](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)](/img/migrated/placeholder.png)

## Step 4: A confirmation dialog appears. Click **Detach** to confirm.
