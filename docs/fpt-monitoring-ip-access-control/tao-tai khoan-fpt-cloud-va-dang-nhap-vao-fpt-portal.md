# Create an FPT Cloud account and log in to FPT Portal

To start using **FPT Monitoring**, you need to log in to **FPT Portal**.

Go to **<https://console.fptcloud.com>** and sign in with your FPT Cloud credentials. Select the correct **Tenant, Region, and VPC** for the service you want to use.

If you do not have an FPT Cloud account, visit the homepage at **[https://fptcloud.com](https://fptcloud.com/)**, click **Sign Up**, and fill in the required information. The support team will contact you to confirm your details and activate your account.

If you encounter any issues during this process, contact our Support team for assistance.
