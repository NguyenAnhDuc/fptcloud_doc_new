# DDoS Protection

DDoS Protection on FPT Cloud helps you detect, block, and automatically respond to Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attacks, ensuring your services remain available and stable.

## Key features

- **Real-time monitoring**: Automatically tracks traffic and detects abnormal behaviour on your network.
- **Automatic defence activation**: Triggers traffic filtering and anti-DDoS mechanisms when an attack is detected.
- **Transparent reporting**: Provides real-time and periodic reports to customers.
