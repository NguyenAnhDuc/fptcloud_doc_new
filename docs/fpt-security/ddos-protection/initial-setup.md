# Initial setup

Complete the following steps before using DDoS Protection.

## Create an FPT Cloud account and log in

1. Visit [https://fptcloud.com/](https://fptcloud.com/) and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter the required information to register. The support team will contact you to confirm your account.
3. Log in at [https://console.fptcloud.com](https://console.fptcloud.com) with your FPT Cloud credentials.
4. Select the correct **Tenant**, **Region**, and **VPC** for your service.

:::tip
If you encounter any issues, contact the support team at hotline **1900 638 399** or email **support@fptcloud.com**.
:::
