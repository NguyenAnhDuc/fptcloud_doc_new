# Manage accounts

**Step 1:** In the **Menu**, select **Manage and Settings** (**Ctrl + 4**) > select **Permissions & Administrators** > click **New**.

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 100](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-100-1-bf21de64.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-100-1-bf21de64.png)

**Step 2:** Enter the account details: **Name, Comment, Password, Permission, Expiration**.

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 101](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-101-fa63b3ee.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-101-fa63b3ee.png)

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 102](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-102-a80b1410.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-102-a80b1410.png)

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 103](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-103-a87a4c8d.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-103-a87a4c8d.png)

**Step 3:** Verify the created account and log in for the first time.

**Step 1:** In the **Menu**, select **Manage and Settings** (**Ctrl + 4**) > select **Permissions & Administrators** > select the account to edit > click **Edit**.

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 104](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-104-1-62f6e8ec.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-104-1-62f6e8ec.png)

**Step 2:** Enter the new information.

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 105](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-105-090b32cf.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-105-090b32cf.png)

**Step 3:** Verify the updated account and log in.

**Step 1:** In the **Menu**, select **Manage and Settings** (**Ctrl + 4**) > select **Permissions & Administrators** > select the account to delete > click **Delete**.

[![Userguide Checkpoint FPT NGFW 2022 106](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-106-1-50c627a0.png)](/img/migrated/Userguide-Checkpoint-FPT-NGFW-2022-106-1-50c627a0.png)

**Step 2:** Confirm the action.
