Log in to WAF Dashboard

**Step 1:** After successfully creating the WAF server, access the inbox of the email address used to create the WAF to retrieve the login credentials for the WAF Dashboard, including the **Dashboard link**, **Username**, and **Password**.

![Userguide FPT WAF 2022 3](/img/migrated/docs-fpt-cloud-waf/dang-nhap-waf-dashboard/img-001.png)

**Step 2:** Access the **Dashboard link** and log in.

![Userguide FPT WAF 2022 4](/img/migrated/docs-fpt-cloud-waf/dang-nhap-waf-dashboard/img-002.png)
